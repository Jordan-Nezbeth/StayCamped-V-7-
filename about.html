👋 Our Story

We’re changing how America camps — one setup at a time. StayCamped was born from a simple idea: camping should be easy, exciting, and accessible. Whether you're new to the outdoors or a lifelong camper, we strive to enhance the experience from both sides.

We give campers a way to enjoy the outdoors without the towing, setup, or storage — and we give camper owners a way to earn extra income by renting their RVs out at real campgrounds.

I grew up in a family-owned camper dealership and saw two major issues:

“I want to camp, but I don’t own a camper.”
“I own a camper, but I hardly use it — and it’s not paying for itself.”

StayCamped solves both. It’s the bridge between campers who want the experience and owners who want a return on their investment.

🚀 Our Mission

To simplify camping for everyone — by connecting people to pre-set RV experiences, empowering owners to earn, and bringing the outdoors within reach.

We’re solving real problems with real experience. Backed by:

  • ✅ A trusted camper network
  • ✅ Real inventory
  • ✅ A founder who’s all-in and growing this from the ground up
